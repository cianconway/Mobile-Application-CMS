Types of physical activity (2)

Anaerobic exercise is short in duration and high in intensity.

If you are gasping for air while talking, you are probably working anaerobically.

When you work anaerobically, you will tire faster and are more likely to experience sore muscles after exercise is over.

Anaerobic activities include, downhill skiing, weight lifting, sprinting, softball, soccer and football.

Both types of activity, aerobic and anaerobic are good for people with depression.
