Physical activity and health (1)

There are many benefits of regular physical activity. You know which ones?

  • Exercise combats chronic diseases. Regular physical activity can help you prevent (or manage) high blood pressure and prevent type 2 diabetes, osteoporosis and certain types of cancer.
