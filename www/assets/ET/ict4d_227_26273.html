Types of physical activity (1)

There are two types of physical activity, namely aerobic exercise and anaerobic exercise.

Aerobic exercise is physical exercise of relatively low intensity and long duration.

Aerobic means with air or oxygen. You should be able to carry on a short conversation while doing aerobic exercise.

Aerobic activities include: walking, biking, jogging, swimming and aerobic classes.
