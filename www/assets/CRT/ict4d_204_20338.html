Step 3: Change yourself

Changing automatic thoughts

The next step is to determine whether there are alternative ways of thinking in a situation. It is necessary that you examine the accuracy of your negative automatic thoughts in a structured and systematic manner. By becoming more skilful in detecting and exploring the thoughts they will feel less significant and easier to deal with. Several practical ways of doing this are presented on the following pages.
