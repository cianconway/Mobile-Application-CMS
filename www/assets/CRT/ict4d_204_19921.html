Core beliefs and rules of life

A person's core beliefs and rules of life are often expressed in three or four words:
  • "I'm unattractive."
  • "I'm fragile."
  • "It's not possible to change."
Our core beliefs act as the foundation for the rules and and attitudes we form, that tell us how we should behave and respond in life:
  • "My value as a person depends on what others think of me."
