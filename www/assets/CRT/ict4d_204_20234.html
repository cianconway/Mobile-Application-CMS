Step 2: Assess yourself

Exercise 2: Credibility

Describe the emotions you experienced in the situation. How did you feel?

Emotions can generally be described in one word, e.g. embarrassed, humiliated, sad, angry, scared.

You may very well experience many emotions at the same time. Also add any physical symptoms that you may experience in the situation, such as palpitations or dizziness.

Indicate how strong the emotions were that you experienced in this situation.
