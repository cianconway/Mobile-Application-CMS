Thoughts, feelings and behaviours

Thoughts, feelings and behaviours are all equally important parts to consider in the treatment.

Our thoughts affect how we feel. But feelings can also affect how we think and behave.

If you find yourself in a situation where you are very nervous it's easy to get negative thoughts ("Help! Others will notice I'm nervous!"). This in turn influences how you behave in the situation.
