About depression (2)

Some people describe depression as a temporary dip that is manageable. Other people have recurring thoughts about death that become unbearable.

Some people recover spontaneously, while others suffer from reoccurring episodes. Some experience it as a continuous gloominess, stretching over years and years.

Common to all of these descriptions is the feeling of being sad and thinking that the future lacks hope.

Depressieve klachten (2)

Daarnaast kunt u last hebben van een of meer van de volgende klachten:

  • geen eetlust, of juist meer eetlust,
  • slecht slapen, of juist meer slapen dan anders,
  • rusteloosheid,
  • langzaam bewegen,
  • vermoeidheid,
  • schuldgevoel,
  • concentratieproblemen,
  • denken aan de dood, of aan zelfdoding.
