Sleeping difficulties

Having difficulties sleeping while being depressed is very common.

Many people lie awake during the nights and ruminate over things that have happened or will happen. Some wake up several times during the night or far too early in the morning.

Some can experience desperation in the prospect of going to bed, since they now how tired they will become if they fail to fall asleep.
