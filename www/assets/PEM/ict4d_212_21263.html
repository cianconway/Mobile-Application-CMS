Overview of the modules

Cognitive restructuring

Here you will learn how your thoughts are connected to your depression. The first part of the module is focused solely on this connection, and you will learn about the concept of negative cognitions/automatic thoughts. An introduction is given on learning how to observe these thoughts, which in turn the second part is building upon. The next step is learning to alter your thinking patterns, i.e. challenging our negative thoughts.
