Continuing with Moodbuster

The previous screens briefly describe each module. Now it is time to choose the module and Moodbuster will support you in this choice. To make sure we can provide you with the best advice, please fill out the end-of-module questionnaire in the PC version of Moodbuster. After you have filled out the questionnaire, the system will provide advice on your treatment. Our advice is that you start with the module with the highest star rating on the treatment module screen. If you don't feel like doing this module right now you can also choose another less highly rated module.
