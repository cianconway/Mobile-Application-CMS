Overview of the modules

Problem-solving therapy

Symptoms of depression are not seldom caused by practical problems in the daily life. This module teaches a specific problem-solving procedure, letting you resolve your problems in a structured way.

You will begin with describing what really matters to you, much like when you established treatment goals.

You will then categorize these problems depending on how important they are and if they are solvable, and receive different strategies to try out accordingly.
