Exercise 2: Your pleasant activity score

Here is your list with the activities you like. Now rate how often these pleasant activities happened to you in the last week and how nice it was for you. For the activities you never did try to think of how much you liked them if you would do them. The scores you receive from this exercise form your own starting point at the beginning of this module. If you do this exercise again over two or three weeks you can see whether your scores are getting higher which indicate that your pleasant activity level is increasing.
