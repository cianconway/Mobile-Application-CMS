Step 3: Change Yourself

Suggestions

Maybe it's difficult for you to carry out your plan. Here are some suggestions to start and continue with your plan.
  • Accept that all beginnings are difficult
  • Accept setbacks and relapse
  • Set simple goals
  • Adjust your plan in case you can't reach it
  • Reward yourself for improvement
