Behavioural Activation (23/30)
Step 3: Change Yourself

Exercise 7: Activities to do more and less often

Let's increase your pleasant activities and decrease your unpleasant activities. Take a look at your list of activities again.
  • Take the ones you like but almost never perform and add these to your calendar.
  • Try to add other or new pleasant activities.
  • Also add other unpleasant activities
  • If necessary, adapt your goal.
  • Add all activities to your calendar.
