Problem Solving Therapy (19/36)
Step 3: Change yourself

Focus on positive thoughts

Train yourself to have positive thoughts.

  1. Write down positive thoughts on i.e. file cards.
  2. Write one positive thought on each card, for example, something you are good at or something you enjoy.
  3. Ask those around you what they think are your strong points, and write these on the cards.
  4. Draw a card at regular intervals (e.g. when you get up in the morning, or after your worry time).
