Step 3: Change yourself

Support Groups

It may feel good to talk with someone who shares similar experiences as you. Therefore, there are support groups. Most people find a lot of positive aspects with a support group.
Getting involved in a group and talk with others in similar situations can give a sense of belonging. You can also get the feeling that you are not alone with your thoughts and feelings, which can be quite nice.
